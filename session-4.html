Pros

  • Builds aerobic strength with the 1km reps.
  • Forces gear changes late in the session (600s).
  • High volume session.

Cons

  • Complex structure (write it on your hand).
  • The short recovery (200m) catches up to you.
Coach's Tip:

The 1000m reps are the "meat and potatoes" of this session. Do not run them faster than 10k pace. If you rush them, you will have nothing left for the final 600m reps where the speed matters.

Session Logic

We start at 5k pace to prime the system, drop to 10k pace to build strength (1000m reps), then ramp up the speed significantly to finish with fast 3k-paced 600s.
